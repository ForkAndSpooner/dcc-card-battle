Here is the data for the major boss encounters from the *Dungeon Crawler Carl* series, perfectly formatted for adapting into your card game. These bosses are ordered by floor and story progression, highlighting their visual aesthetics, mechanics, and thematic rewards.

### 1\. The Hoarder

* **Floor/Area:** 1st Floor / Garbage Dump Neighborhood 1, 2  
* **Visual Description:** A towering, garbage-troll giantess. Half of her face is burned to hell, and she wears a broken dog leash around her neck. She is surrounded by a 15-foot-tall labyrinth of stinking garbage bags, fast-food wrappers, and trash 1, 3, 4\.  
* **Personality/Voice:** Tragic, sobbing, and desperate. Her mind has been broken by the dungeon, and she cries in Spanish about not wanting to go to hell, even as she attacks 1, 5\.  
* **Signature Mechanic:** **Choking Swarm.** When she opens her mouth, she violently vomits out giant cockroach monsters. If a player attacks the bugs *while* they are in her mouth before they fully emerge, it causes her to choke and take massive internal damage 4, 6\.  
* **Minions:** Scatterer Brood Guardians (massive, highly aggressive cockroaches "baptized in rage and Monster Energy drinks") 4, 7\.  
* **Intro Line:** *"Lo siento si fui una mala persona... ¡No quiero estar en el infierno\!"* (I'm sorry if I was a bad person... I don't want to be in hell\!) 5  
* **Threat Level:** 3/10 (High for level 1, but mechanically easy to exploit).  
* **Thematic Reward:** Neighborhood Map and the "Boss Babe" Achievement 5, 8\.

### 2\. Krakaren Clone

* **Floor/Area:** 2nd Floor / Rev-Up Syndicate Base 9, 10  
* **Visual Description:** A collective mind entity featuring massive, 15-foot-long pink tentacles. Instead of suckers, the tentacles are covered in dozens of frisbee-sized, human-shaped mouths with bright red lips and sharp teeth 9, 11\.  
* **Personality/Voice:** An ear-splitting cacophony of shrieking voices saying absolutely nothing—just pure, anti-intellectual rage 9, 11\.  
* **Signature Mechanic:** **Hydra's Reach.** The boss never fully enters the room; instead, its tentacles smash blindly through the walls and ceilings from adjacent chambers. For every one killed, the Krakaren Prime births two more 9, 11, 12\.  
* **Minions:** Clurichaun Consultants (vicious leprechauns with slingshots) and Brindle Grubs (corpse-eating worms) 11-13.  
* **Intro Line:** *An ear-splitting, multi-mouthed shriek of pure, unadulterated rage that shakes the walls* 11\.  
* **Threat Level:** 5/10.  
* **Thematic Reward:** Rev-Up Toilet-Grade Moonshine and a massive surge of Brindle Grubs 10, 13\.

### 3\. Heather the Roller-Skating Bear

* **Floor/Area:** 3rd Floor / Grimaldi's Traveling Circus (Over City) 14  
* **Visual Description:** A black bear forced into a pink tutu. She has writhing worms for claws, and her back feet have literal roller skates magically fused to them 15, 16\.  
* **Personality/Voice:** Silent, relentless, and tragic. She has brief flashes of lucidity where she remembers she always hated the circus attendees anyway 14, 16\.  
* **Signature Mechanic:** **Skating Charge.** She uses unnatural speed on her roller skates to run down fleeing prey, using momentum to grapple crawlers and drag them back to feed the clowns 14, 16\.  
* **Minions:** Mold Lions (mean, green, undead lions from the circus) 17, 18\.  
* **Intro Line:** *"Good. I’ve always hated all you assholes, anyway."* (Spoken in an internal monologue as she charges) 16\.  
* **Threat Level:** 6/10.  
* **Thematic Reward:** Neighborhood Map and Heather's powerful blood (used by NPCs for summoning rituals) 19, 20\.

### 4\. Ringmaster Grimaldi (Pestiferous Vine)

* **Floor/Area:** 3rd Floor / Grimaldi's Circus Center (Over City) 21  
* **Visual Description:** A towering, massive plant monster that is as tall as a circus tent. It acts as the core of the circus, with writhing vines and parasitic worms pulsing from its central stalk 21, 22\.  
* **Personality/Voice:** The ultimate corrupted patriarch. He loves his circus "family" and uses mind-control spores to force them to protect him at all costs 21\.  
* **Signature Mechanic:** **Parasitic Spores.** Controls all elites and mobs in the circus area. Players must bypass the physical vines and attack its core directly; ironically, using *healing* spells on its roots can sometimes disrupt its mechanics 22, 23\.  
* **Minions:** Mind-controlled circus freaks, Blood and Ink elementals, and a legion of clowns 20, 22, 24\.  
* **Intro Line:** *"When the cataclysm came, I remained center stage. And I will remain here forever."* 21  
* **Threat Level:** 8/10 (City Boss).  
* **Thematic Reward:** A massive Experience payout and the survival of the circus elites 23, 25\.

### 5\. The Mimic Rex

* **Floor/Area:** 4th Floor / The Iron Tangle (Station 433\) 26  
* **Visual Description:** A Level 90 City Boss that initially takes the shape of a massive railway administrative building. When it attacks, the entire first floor yawns open into a 300-foot-wide mouth filled with person-sized, jagged teeth and a lumpy, train-sized red tongue 26\.  
* **Personality/Voice:** The silent, ravenous apex predator of the tangle. It views crawlers simply as crunchy snacks 27\.  
* **Signature Mechanic:** **Chomp & Split.** If you chop a piece off the boss, the severed piece grows centipede legs and scuttles back to heal the main body. Players must sever over 50% of its mass simultaneously to disable its healing and make it vulnerable 26, 28\.  
* **Minions:** Car-sized mouths with centipede legs (literally severed pieces of its own body) 29\.  
* **Intro Line:** *The sound of grinding concrete and screeching steel as an entire building unfolds into a fleshy maw* 26, 27\.  
* **Threat Level:** 10/10 (City Boss).  
* **Thematic Reward:** The Iron Tangle Route Map 30\.

### 6\. Lusca (Octo-Shark Brood Mother Queen)

* **Floor/Area:** 5th Floor / The Bubbles (Water Quadrant) 31  
* **Visual Description:** A colossal Sharktopus (half great white shark, half giant octopus). Her gaping mouth is lined with jagged teeth the size of surfboards 31\.  
* **Personality/Voice:** An enraged, primal force of nature—an apex sea monster defending her territory 31\.  
* **Signature Mechanic:** **Soft Vore.** She swallows opponents whole, trapping them inside her massive stomach. Players must fight their way out from the inside before being fully digested or crushed 32\.  
* **Minions:** Concierge Sharks (lightning-fast psycho sharks that will eat literally anything, drawn by the scent of blood) 33\.  
* **Intro Line:** *A deafening underwater roar that shakes the ocean floor, followed by the rush of millions of gallons of displaced water* 31\.  
* **Threat Level:** 9/10 (City Boss).  
* **Thematic Reward:** Platinum Spicy Box (Awarded specifically for escaping her stomach via the "Soft Vore" achievement) 32\.

### 7\. Queen Imogen

* **Floor/Area:** 6th Floor / The Hunting Grounds (High Elf Castle) 34  
* **Visual Description:** A breathtakingly beautiful High Elf Cleric Sorceress in regal, flowing attire 34\. When attacked or moving, she can turn completely intangible, dissipating like dandelion seeds caught in a breeze 35\.  
* **Personality/Voice:** Cold, arrogant, and highly manipulative. She views the crawlers as mere insects trespassing in her court and resents the Dungeon's games 36, 37\.  
* **Signature Mechanic:** **Intangibility & Reflection.** She is initially immune to physical touch. She is immune to poison and curses, and automatically reflects magical fire back at attackers. She must be hit with synchronized blunt-force attacks to the exact beat of the background music to take damage 34, 38, 39\.  
* **Minions:** High Elf Guards, Ice Elves, and magical automated Castle Defenses 40, 41\.  
* **Intro Line:** *"My castle is besieged. You’re trying to ruin my spell... You are nothing but insects in my court."* 37  
* **Threat Level:** 10/10 (Country Boss).  
* **Thematic Reward:** The Treasure Map and access to loot the High Elf Castle vault 42, 43\.

### 8\. The Reminiscence Hydra of Malicious Compliance

* **Floor/Area:** 8th Floor / Ghosts of Earth (Cuba/Trailer Park) 44, 45  
* **Visual Description:** A grotesque, building-sized hydra where each of the nine heads is a human face plucked from Carl's memories (like his awful boss Dick, and even the tiny head of a cat named King Croissant). The main body is fleshy and nearly indestructible 44-46.  
* **Personality/Voice:** Screaming constantly. Each head embodies different memories, insecurities, and insults, acting as a grotesque psychological weapon designed to break the crawler's will 47, 48\.  
* **Signature Mechanic:** **Adaptive Head Regeneration.** Each head provides the main body with a specific magical resistance and attack (e.g., fire, lightning). If a head is cut off, it inflates back out of the neck stump like a balloon. Players must cleanly decapitate it and cauterize the stump immediately to prevent regeneration 46\.  
* **Minions:** Card Totems drawn from a specialized deck (including "Alpha Male Carl", "Leveled-Up Frank", and "King Croissant") 49-51.  
* **Intro Line:** *"I'm gonna get to you in a minute, Lesser Carl. We gotta have a talk about how you treat family. We’re gonna have some fist therapy."* (Spoken by the Alpha Carl head/totem) 49\.  
* **Threat Level:** 10/10 (City Boss).  
* **Thematic Reward:** Totem Cards of the defeated memory ghosts (Alpha Carl, Leveled-Up Frank) 52, 53\.

### 9\. Shi Maria (The Bedlam Bride)

* **Floor/Area:** 8th Floor / Cuba (Can also be summoned as a Mythic Card) 54, 55  
* **Visual Description:** A Reaper Spider Minion and demi-god. She has a massive, bulbous spider body with long, horrifying legs that bend and snap metal structures as she squeezes through hallways. She often hides her face behind a coy facade 54, 56\.  
* **Personality/Voice:** Extremely cunning and manipulative. She hides her insidious nature behind a shy "MaeMae" persona, but derives immense, almost sexual pleasure from committing extreme violence 55, 57, 58\.  
* **Signature Mechanic:** **Permanent Bedlam & Cockroach.** Her unique attacks inflict *Permanent Insanity* and *Permanent Blindness*. Furthermore, she possesses a Level 15 "Cockroach" skill, saving her from fatal blows, providing a shield, and fully healing her mid-fight 54, 57\.  
* **Minions:** None inherently, but she actively manipulates other crawlers/spirits (like Samantha) to act as a meat shield or do her bidding 57\.  
* **Intro Line:** *"Injure me just enough to cast the card magic. Do not attempt to slay me. Many have tried. All have failed. Be a friend to me, and I will be a friend to you."* 55  
* **Threat Level:** 11/10 (City Boss / Demi-God).  
* **Thematic Reward:** Becomes a Mythic Totem Card to be used in the player's deck 59\.
