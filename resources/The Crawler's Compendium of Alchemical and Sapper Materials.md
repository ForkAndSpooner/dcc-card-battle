Here are the item cards for the crafting materials featured in the sources, ordered from the most prominently featured to the least.  
**Toraline Root Vegetable (Toraline Yam)**

* **Brief History:** Originally won by Carl in a silver box, this rare tuber was initially thought to be useless until Mordecai and the severed head of Samantha discovered its true value 1, 2\. It was later used to save the crawler Chris from parasitic possession 2, 3\.  
* **Brief Description:** A rare, sweet potato-like tuber that only grows in dirt covered by lava 1\. It tastes awful 1\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Very Rare 4\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Highly versatile in advanced alchemy and scroll-making. It can be brewed into *Phase Lava Rock* potions, specialty healing salves for scaled creatures, or processed into a highly potent magical ink 1, 2\. When mixed with a crawler's blood, the resulting ink can be used to permanently steal spells or skills from unwilling fallen crawlers and tattoo them onto a willing subject 5, 6\.

**Rev-Up Toilet-Grade Moonshine**

* **Brief History:** Hoarded by Carl after raiding the Laminak elites on the second floor, this highly flammable liquid became the base accelerant for his custom "Carl's Jug O' Boom" 7-9.  
* **Brief Description:** A 180-proof, highly flammable liquid housed in clay jugs 8, 10, 11\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** High Value / Dungeon Consumable 11, 12\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Primarily used as an accelerant for thermobaric jug bombs or as a base for alchemical burn gels and immunity smoothies 9, 13, 14\. If consumed straight, it inflicts severe intoxication, potentially causing blindness or death, but offers a small chance to permanently raise a random stat 7\.

**Hobgoblin Pus**

* **Brief History:** First looted from a goblin workshop, Carl utilized this rare explosive to rig a trap that ultimately killed a war chieftain 15, 16\.  
* **Brief Description:** A moldable, C4-like explosive material that must be detonated with an actual trigger 15\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** High Value / Sapper Material 15\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Exceptionally stable compared to standard dynamite, it causes massive, high-yield explosions when paired with a magical or mechanical detonator 15\.

**Nitro Sludge**

* **Brief History:** A chemical by-product discovered by Mordecai while brewing shield potions, Carl utilized it to amplify his massive "Just Wait Until Your Daddy Gets Home" bomb 17, 18\.  
* **Brief Description:** Small square blocks that resemble uncooked ramen noodles 17\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Crafted Alchemical By-Product 17\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Completely inert on its own, but acts as a massive explosion amplifier when vaporized at high temperatures 17\. It releases an insane amount of oxygen, effectively multiplying the explosive force of ammonium nitrate-like materials 17\. If too little or too much is used, it can mute the explosion instead 17\.

**Dwarven Batteries**

* **Brief History:** Looted from inactive industrial automatons and purchased from a moleman shopkeeper 19, 20\. Carl utilized them to power his Chariot vehicle and various heavy saws 21, 22\.  
* **Brief Description:** A metal block about the size and weight of a brick 20, 23\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** High Value / Industrial Power Source 19, 20\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Stores a massive electrical/magical charge capable of running automatons and heavy dungeon vehicles for long periods 23\. Requires a specific fabricator and four full mana potions to charge a single battery 20\.

**Soul Crystals**

* **Brief History:** Carl harvested an overloaded, C-quality crystal from Miss Quill and used it to craft a highly unstable nuclear-equivalent bomb (Carl's Doomsday Scenario) 24\.  
* **Brief Description:** Glowing gems mined from Scolopendra's lair, created when certain souls filter through the centipede's body 25\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Extremely Rare / High Value 24, 25\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Used as heavy power sources to run elite automaton guards, ghoul generators, and other massive dungeon systems 24\. If overloaded, they become violently unstable, detonating with enough force to level an entire city 26\.

**Proximity Triggers**

* **Brief History:** Carl received a pack of ten in a Gold Sapper's Box as a reward for his "You call that a trap?" achievement 27-29.  
* **Brief Description:** A tiny black box the size of a dime with a long length of wire attached 29\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Highly Valuable / Rare Sapper Material 29\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Allows a trapmaker to attach advanced activation conditions (such as countdowns or specific mob-type triggers) to any non-static trap using a Sapper's Table 29\.

**Surefire**

* **Brief Description:** Small, black items about the size of a bottle cap 30\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Gold Sniper's Box Prize 30\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Adds a "Guided" status to projectiles or traps 30, 31\. When attached to a trap, it unlocks advanced targeting protocols (like specifically targeting healers). When attached to rockets or arrows, the projectile will seek a designated target upon firing 31\.

**Fail Safes**

* **Brief Description:** A pack of twenty trap add-ons 32\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Trapmaster Box Prize 32\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** When attached to a trap, they send a direct notification to the user if the trap is ever disabled or set off 32\.

**Sage Beetle Ichor**

* **Brief Description:** A specialized alchemical additive utilized by Mordecai 33\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Crafted / Alchemical Exploit 33\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Disguises potions, allowing them to bypass system protections (such as battle arenas where health potions are normally banned) 33\. The downside is that it causes the treated potion to spoil and go bad after exactly one hour 33\.

**Automaton Parts (Main Driveshafts)**

* **Brief History:** Looted and purchased by Carl to construct "Spider Stalkers" (robot rot stickers) 34\.  
* **Brief Description:** Various mechanical components (cogs, springs, driveshafts) needed for the Automaton Table 34\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Rare / Expensive 34\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Required to assemble custom automatons for reconnaissance, base defense, or combat 34\.

**Blessed Water (Emberus Shrine)**

* **Brief History:** Mordecai harvested blood-red water from the guild's Emberus shrine to craft weapons against vampires 35, 36\.  
* **Brief Description:** Blood-red water imbued with light-based divine power 36\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Guild Amenity Resource 35\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** When mixed with a fine healing potion and a coagulant inside a potion ball, it creates "Holy Goopers" that act as devastating holy napalm against undead and vampires 36\.

**Laminak Fairy Corpse**

* **Brief History:** Crushed headfirst into a bicycle blender by Carl during a desperate boss fight 13\.  
* **Brief Description:** The warm, pudgy body of a dead Rev-Up Consultant Elite 13\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Alchemy Material 37\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** When violently blended with toilet-grade moonshine, it creates a sparkling pink smoothie granting disease immunity (and severe intoxication) 13\.

**Sheol Brick**

* **Brief History:** Looted from a bloody lockbox in a train cab 38\.  
* **Brief Description:** A black chunk of material supposedly reserved for history's greatest villains 38\. It is actually a disguised baby corpse 39\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Rare 39\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Burns significantly hotter and longer than regular coal, lasting until the end of the user's lifetime 38\.

**Brindle Grub Hemolymph**

* **Brief History:** Collected from a bug crushed by Agatha's shopping cart 40\.  
* **Brief Description:** A vial of bug goo 40\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Alchemy Material 40\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Used as a raw material for alchemical crafting 40\.

**Satch Toad Extract**

* **Brief History:** Identified by Mordecai as the countermeasure to Signet's sleeping curse during the circus siege 41, 42\.  
* **Brief Description:** An alchemical extract available in large towns 41, 42\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Common Alchemical Ingredient 41, 42\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** When mixed with a standard healing potion, it completely negates Naiad sleep effects and curses 41, 42\.

**Thistle Rot & Cinnamon Stick**

* **Brief History:** Stolen from the Belly Rubbed Pug's kitchens and used to save Mongo from severe poisoning 43, 44\.  
* **Brief Description:** Standard herbs and spices 43, 44\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Common / Food Supplies 43, 44\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** When mixed into a healing potion in a specific sequence, it acts as a powerful poison cure for pets 43, 44\.

**Gooey Weed & Rubbing Alcohol**

* **Brief History:** Brewed by Mordecai and Carl to cure Louis, Firas, and Donut of extreme intoxication 45-47.  
* **Brief Description:** A weed that smells like okra and standard rubbing alcohol 45, 46\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Common Ingredients 45, 46\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** When mixed into a Hair of the Dog potion, it rapidly clears the system of alcohol and prevents the user from getting drunk for the remainder of the floor 45, 46\.
