**The Dungeon Anarchist’s Cookbook**

* **Brief History:** This guide to creating chaos was originally generated during the fifteenth season and awarded to the High Elf Crawler Porthus on the ninth floor. It has since been passed down to worthy recipients across seasons, currently residing with Carl in its 24th Edition 1\.  
* **Brief Description:** Disguised as a blank sketchbook or a book of "Chicken and Goblin recipes galore," this unique book is actually a massive compilation of recipes, secrets, and lore curated by past crawlers 1-3.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Unique 2\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** It provides invaluable instructions for crafting potions, explosives, traps, and discovering dungeon shortcuts 1\. It grants the owner an extra page in their mental scratchpad to record notes that will be passed on to the 25th edition 4\. If the showrunners suspect the crawler is using it, or if the crawler tells anyone about it, the book will permanently erase itself 4, 5\.

**Scroll of Upgrade**

* **Brief History:** Carl received these on a few occasions, notably using one to upgrade his boxers (granting him the *Freeballing* benefit) and using another won from a roulette wheel to upgrade his Splatter Skunk toe ring 6-9.  
* **Brief Description:** A magical scroll with a short shelf life (20 minutes) that forces the user to apply it almost immediately 8, 10\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** High / Rare (Awarded from Platinum Quest Boxes and Casino wheels) 8, 11\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** When read, it permanently upgrades one random, currently-equipped item 8, 10\. The upgrade can be minor or massive, but attempting to exploit it by unequipping other items often results in poor upgrades 6, 8, 10\.

**Tome of Wisp Armor**

* **Brief History:** Carl obtained this early from a Bronze Boss Box 12\. He hesitated to use it because he wanted to sell it, but eventually learned the spell to survive the Over City 13, 14\.  
* **Brief Description:** A magical tome teaching a protection spell that makes the user look "wispy and ethereal and druid-like" 15\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Bronze Boss Box Prize / Highly Valuable 12, 13\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Teaches the *Wisp Armor* spell. The spell costs 5 mana and surrounds the body with tendrils of light, negating 75% of incoming magic-based damage and providing temporary immunity to mind-control effects 15, 16\.

**The Filthy Little Crawler’s Book of Voodoo (and Carl's Book of Boom)**

* **Brief History:** Distributed to eligible crawlers on the eighth floor to facilitate communication. Later, departing crawlers used the book to draw out their unlearned skills and pass them on, turning Carl's second volume into *Carl's Book of Boom* 17-19.  
* **Brief Description:** A heavy, black leather diary filled with hundreds of blank pages 20\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Distributed to eligible crawlers 21\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** When two crawlers carrying the book meet, their names are permanently added to each other's books 22\. Users can write private messages under a specific crawler's name or public messages under their own name 22\. Additionally, departing crawlers can permanently sacrifice their skills to draw them into the book; whoever consumes the drawn page gains the skill for a short duration 18\.

**Tome of Magic Missile**

* **Brief History:** This was Donut's very first spellbook, received moments after she officially entered the dungeon as a crawler 23, 24\.  
* **Brief Description:** A magical tome that permanently teaches the user a projectile spell 24\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Silver Adventurer Box Prize 23\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Teaches *Magic Missile*, a spell that shoots highly accurate, damaging magical projectiles 25\. It scales in power, allowing Donut to shoot multiple missiles simultaneously depending on the mana points she chooses to allocate 25\.

**Tome of You’re Not Done Yet**

* **Brief History:** Donut received this terrifying, foul-smelling book in a Legendary Quest box 26\. The War Mages on the ninth floor heavily abused this spell to create armies of Fleshers and Symbiotes 27, 28\.  
* **Brief Description:** A black-hued tome that clanks heavily when set down and smells exactly like purposely burned microwave popcorn 26\. Purple wisps of smoke constantly rise from it 26\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Legendary Quest Box Prize 26\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Teaches a highly insidious necromancy spell that rips the loose skin off a fallen soldier to create a sentient minion known as a Flesher 29, 30\. The Flesher then seeks out bones to "re-sleeve" itself into a powerful symbiote 30\.

**Tome of Minion Army**

* **Brief History:** Donut received this early in the dungeon from a Beguiler Box, but she couldn't read it for several floors because her mana pool was too low 31, 32\. She finally read it after raising her Intelligence high enough 33, 34\.  
* **Brief Description:** A magical tome teaching a battlefield sabotage spell 34\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Beguiler Box Prize 32\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Teaches *Minion Army*. Costing a massive 50 spell points and taking five minutes to cast, this spell turns a percentage of intelligent enemies in a wide area against their own comrades 31, 34\.

**Scroll of Size Up**

* **Brief History:** Miriam Dom owned over forty of these scrolls, using them to slowly read the stats of massive boss monsters 35, 36\. Carl stole a few from her stash to use later 36\.  
* **Brief Description:** A consumable informational scroll 36, 37\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Rare / Expensive 36, 37\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** When read, it takes about 30 seconds for the information to seep in, revealing the exact stats, skills, and specific weaknesses of targeted mobs and bosses 35, 37\.

**Scroll of Community Pool**

* **Brief History:** Prepotente received this in a Benefactor box and used it during the fight against Viscount Fog. The spell violently bounced back and forth, ultimately killing his owner, Miriam Dom 38-40.  
* **Brief Description:** A highly powerful affliction-transferring scroll 39\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Benefactor Box Prize 39\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Requires the caster to physically touch two parties. It takes an infection or debuff from one party and forcefully transfers it to the other 39\.

**Scroll of Heal / Scroll of Heal Critter**

* **Brief History:** Awarded heavily in early loot boxes. Carl frequently used them to heal Donut and himself in desperate situations without waiting for potion cooldowns 41-43.  
* **Brief Description:** A basic, rolled-up restorative scroll 41, 44\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Common (Often from Silver Survivor's Boxes or Bronze Pet Boxes) 44, 45\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Instantly heals the target. Unlike potions, the scrolls have no cooldown timer between uses and can be cast on other party members 45\.

**Scroll of Confusing Fog**

* **Brief History:** Carl used these early on to blind mobs, but his low Intelligence meant the fog only lasted 15 seconds per use 46\. He later learned to let Donut cast them 46\.  
* **Brief Description:** A crowd-control scroll 47\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Common (Gold Crowd Control Box / Silver Quest Box) 12, 47\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Fills the immediate area with a thick mist that only mobs can see, blinding them 48\. The duration of the fog scales directly with the caster's Intelligence stat 46\.

**Mysterious Letter (Scroll of Suppurating Eye)**

* **Brief History:** Found on the corpse of GumGum the Orc, planted purposely by Magistrate Featherfall to spy on Carl and Donut 49-52.  
* **Brief Description:** A folded piece of paper stained red with blood, written in squiggly and triangular necroscript 50, 53\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Uncommon Lich Magic 52\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** *Suppurating Eye* is a spy spell. The caster leaks bodily fluid (blood) onto the scroll; once the scroll is removed from an inventory, it acts as a camera and microphone, allowing the caster to see and hear everything around it 52\.

**Tome of Legionnaires of the Damned**

* **Brief History:** Received by Donut in a De-Sleeving Box. The book was so potent it physically bounced around on the table 54\.  
* **Brief Description:** A thick, bumpy, leather-bound book that is chained shut with a lock. It emits purple smoke, wriggles like a puppy, and screams when read 54-56.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** High Value / De-Sleeving Box Prize 54\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Teaches a summoning spell that reanimates recently unequipped armor and weapons. It plucks wailing spirits from the river of falling souls and binds them to the gear, turning them into temporary symbiote minions that attack enemies 57, 58\.

**Tome of Bitch, What?**

**Scroll of Build Trench / Fill Trench / Temporary Water Source**

* **Brief History:** Given out like candy in "Silver Spam Boxes" to crawlers on the 8th floor to prepare for Faction Wars. Armies heavily spammed these to navigate the battlefield, drown enemies, and bypass magical barriers 61-63.  
* **Brief Description:** Tactical terrain-altering scrolls 64\.
