**Emberus**

* **Brief Backstory:** The Sun and Ash God 1\. He is the youngest brother of Taranis and half of the sun duology 1\. Emberus feels he is the best-suited candidate to ascend to the Celestial Throne, but he is usually an unmitigated asshole who burns everyone just for looking at him funny 1\. He plucked his own eyes out in grief when his son, Geyrun, was murdered 2\.

* **Visual Description:** A massive, humanoid god who is asymmetrical from behind. A massive, curved, smoking horn erupts from the left side of his head, while his right side is caved in 7\. His skin is made of curls of orange and red fire that vaguely form a muscular body, though he sometimes appears as shimmering, smoldering rocky gray with multicolor hotspots 7, 8\. His empty eye sockets trail smoke and rain blood, and he has a secondary, glowing-red, screaming mouth on his right cheek 8\.

### The Ascended Orishas

*These former gods were made flesh upon the rise of the Ascendency, fading into non-corporeal spirits until they are resurrected 128\.*

* **Oshun:** Sweet, sweet Oshun. A beautiful, dark-skinned woman in a flowing, yellow dress made of ethereal light 129, 130\. She grants massive charisma boosts (+50%), protects from 5 fatal blows, and gives the *Heal Party* spell 130\. Her voice is seductive and whispers directly into the mind 131, 132\.  
* **Legba:** The ghost of an older man wearing a fedora, casually leaning against the wall and flipping a coin. He has a rooster on his shoulder 133\. He grants a 50% chance to avoid smites and the *Confusing Fog* spell 133\. His voice is casual and shouts 134\.  
* **Shango:** A shy Orisha. He did not manifest a ghost, remaining just a pile of rocks 133\. Repairing his shrine grants a level-15 *Lightning* spell 133\.  
* **Asojano:** Lord of Smallpox. One of the most respected and feared Orishas 128, 130\. An undead spirit who wields a short staff with a sugar skull at the end and wears a grass skirt (which turns to woven gold in his combo form) 135\. Controls disease 130\.  
* **Inle:** A former healing god who spent time in the Nothing, rendering him "batshit" crazy 136\. He appears as a well-muscled, androgynous ghost with a translucent sea serpent twisted around his body 136\. He grants immunity from health-seeping conditions and \+50% Constitution 136\. He is deaf and mute, but speaks telepathically in the mind 132\.
