Here are the item cards for the weapons featured in the sources, ordered from the most prominently featured to the least.  
**Enchanted War Gauntlet of the Exalted Grull (Right-Handed)**

* **Brief History:** Won from a Gold Brawler box by Carl early in the dungeon, this bracer became his signature and primary melee weapon 1, 2\.  
* **Brief Description:** A charcoal-colored wrist bracer that transforms into a heavy, spiked war gauntlet made of orcish steel 2\. It activates with a haptic buzz when the user forms a fist for two seconds, and vanishes in a puff of smoke when the fist is released 2, 3\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** High Value / Gold-tier Box Prize 1\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Adds \+3 Strength (in fist mode) and \+1 Dexterity 2\. Grants \+2 Skill Levels to Iron Punch and \+1 Skill Level to Powerful Strike 2\. It has a 2% chance to stun an enemy upon a successful hit, and uniquely, it does not negate Carl's bare-knuckle skill bonus 2\. It carries a dangerous 1.5% chance to transfigure a target into the war god Grull if used to strike one of his adherents 2\.

**Xistera Scoop (Clurichaun Slingshot Replacement)**

* **Brief History:** Gifted to Carl in a Platinum Fan Box to replace his ineffective Clurichaun slingshot 4-7.  
* **Brief Description:** A mechanical, banana-shaped wicker basket scoop (based on the sport of jai-alai) that attaches to the arm. Pulling a strap ring causes it to twist and retract smoothly into a metallic bracer that perfectly fits under his Grull war gauntlet 4-6.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Platinum Fan Box Prize 4\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Allows the user to throw round explosives (like Hob-Lobbers) and potion balls with incredible accuracy, hardness, and speed 5, 8, 9\.

**Enchanted Repeating Crossbow of the Scavenger Mother of Mothers (Nekhebit's Crossbow)**

* **Brief History:** Originally a divine weapon wielded by Hekla, it was looted by Katia after Katia accidentally crushed Hekla on a train 10-12. It was later mounted onto Donut's back 13\.  
* **Brief Description:** A massive, golden automatic crossbow inlaid with vulture carvings 12\. When mounted on a quadruped, it connects via a "Scorpion's Bite" mount 13\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Unique / Divine Item 14\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** It acts as a "ranged chainsaw" with an unlimited basic ammo buffet enchantment 10, 14\. It must be wielded by a female, and increases its damage and firing speed by 25% for every female in the party 15\. The weapon adds \+15 Dexterity and \+10 Strength to the user, and base damage scales with the user's strength and level 15\. It also casts *Birth Defect* on monster types that generate or birth other monsters 15\.

**Carl’s Jug O’ Boom**

* **Brief History:** Crafted by Carl, earning him the "Dungeonpreneur" achievement for inventing a stackable weapon 16\.  
* **Brief Description:** A custom incendiary tossable weapon crafted from a jug, goblin oil, gunpowder, and a torch 17, 18\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Custom Crafted / Stackable Weapon 16, 18\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** When lit and tossed, it causes a massive thermobaric explosion followed by burning splash damage over a wide area 17, 18\. The flames burn for 15 seconds per level of the user's Incendiary Device Handling skill 18\.

**Spunky Jefferson (The Enchanted Nickel Sock of the Elderly Miser)**

* **Brief History:** Originally from a Legendary box, it was hoarded by the player-killer Quan Ch before Carl looted it and gifted it to the elderly stripper Dong Quixote. Dong reverently treats it as a mythical knight's weapon and frequently listens to it speak 19-25.  
* **Brief Description:** A crusty, blood-splattered white tube sock filled with several dollars' worth of nickels 19, 22, 25\. It is a sapient weapon that speaks into the mind of its wielder 24\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Legendary / Mythical Sapient Weapon 19, 22, 25\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Its damage increases incrementally by 1.5% for every mob it kills (if the mob is within 10 levels of the wielder) 19, 23\. It boosts the wielder's Dexterity by 10 points plus 10%, has a 5% chance to inflict the *Bonked* debuff, and gives a 20% chance for killed enemies to burst into a pile of gold coins upon death 19, 24\.

**Carl’s Doomsday Scenario**

* **Brief History:** Created by Carl in a desperate attempt to survive a lich, he combined a massively overloaded soul crystal with a Sheol Glass Reaper Case 26-29.  
* **Brief Description:** A blindingly bright, caged miniature sun inside a glass case 28\. It is incredibly unstable and detonates the moment it is removed from the inventory 27, 29\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Unique / Unstable Custom Explosive 26, 28\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Causes an explosion large enough to "rattle the teeth of a god" and is capable of leveling an entire city and all of its surrounding suburbs 26-29.

**Hobgoblin Hob-Lobber**

**Enchanted Fang Caps of the Expectorating Tizheruk**
