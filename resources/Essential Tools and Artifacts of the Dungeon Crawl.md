Here are the item cards for the Tools and Utility Artifacts featured in the sources, ordered from the most prominently featured to the least.  
**The Filthy Little Crawler’s Book of Voodoo**

* **Brief History:** Distributed to eligible crawlers on the eighth floor to facilitate communication during the Faction Wars 1\. Later, departing crawlers used the book's mechanics to draw out their unlearned skills and pass them on 1, 2\.  
* **Brief Description:** A heavy, black leather diary filled with hundreds of blank pages 1, 3\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Distributed Artifact (Cannot be traded or destroyed) 1\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** It acts as a magical communication device. When two crawlers carrying the book meet, their names are permanently added to each other's books 3, 4\. Users can write private messages under a specific crawler's name or public messages under their own name 2, 3\. It also synergizes with specialized ink, allowing crawlers to temporarily or permanently share unlearned spells and skills 5, 6\.

**Enchanted Pedicure Kit of the Sylph**

* **Brief History:** Received by Carl early on. The buffs ended up saving his life when he was struck by an explosive table trap; his legs were broken instead of severed completely at the ankles 7, 8\.  
* **Brief Description:** A black, zippered folder containing 12 miniature metal foot-care tools (from a pumice stone to a cuticle pusher) 9-12.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Boss Box Prize 9, 11\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** When used for nightly foot care within a Safe Room or Personal Space, it grants 30-hour buffs (but requires the user to remain barefoot): \+15% Damage to barefoot attacks, \+3 to the Smush skill, Unbreakable feet (protecting metatarsals), and "Celestially Nimble and Tidy" (traps set off by footfalls trigger an alarm with a 5-second delay before detonating) 10, 12-14.

**Goo-Inator 3000**

* **Brief History:** Awarded to Carl from a Silver Mechanic's Box. He used it to shape pieces of heavy metal, including thick swordsman breastplates, into usable custom armor items and backpacks 15-17.  
* **Brief Description:** A shaping tool that looks like a silver, oversized lollipop with a button on the handle 15, 18\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Silver Mechanic's Box Prize 15, 19, 20\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** A tool that must be used at a workbench. It can manipulate, flatten, and freeform heavy materials (like solid iron) as if they were dough. However, it is not very precise and has a tendency to lower the overall material strength of the item if overused 15, 16, 21\.

**Sheol Glass Reaper Case**

* **Brief History:** Carl originally found his rare Kimaris figure housed in one. He later repurposed the empty case to house his massively overloaded soul crystal, creating "Carl's Doomsday Scenario" 22-24.  
* **Brief Description:** A small, glass protective display case with a hinged flap 22, 24\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Expensive Artifact 22, 24\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Forged in the fires of the 15th level (Sheol) and sold by traveling Spider Reaper Minions, it acts as an ultimate protective barrier that can safely house incredibly dangerous, highly valuable, or highly volatile items 22, 24\.

**Charge Pillow (for the Tiara of a Thousand Lights)**

* **Brief History:** Donut received the red one with her Tiara, and a purple one shortly after in another Benefactor box 25, 26\.  
* **Brief Description:** A small, colored pillow (red or purple) 26, 27\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Celestial / Benefactor Box Prize 25, 26\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** If an enchanted hat is placed on the pillow for 10 hours, it safely drains all magical buffs from the hat (destroying the hat's magic but preserving its physical appearance for collections). The pillow stores the magic until the Tiara is placed on it, permanently transferring and consolidating the buffs into the Tiara 27, 28\.

**Coffee Shop Author Kit**

* **Brief History:** Awarded in a Gold "Makeup Sex is the Best Sex" box. Carl used it to secretly back up pages of the *Dungeon Anarchist's Cookbook* without the AI detecting it 29, 30\.  
* **Brief Description:** A magical sheet of paper, a quill pen, and a capped jar of black ink 30, 31\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Gold Box Prize (Pricey) 30, 32\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Anything written or drawn on the magical paper transfers directly to the user's mental scratchpad in their interface, and vice versa. It can also be used by scholar classes to write and sell functional magical scrolls 31, 33\.

**Valtay Corporation Neural Enhancer \#544 (Variant 32.c)**

* **Brief History:** Gifted to Carl in a Bronze Benefactor box by the Valtay Corporation. They sent it to give him an edge in disrupting the Borant Corporation's crawl 34-37.  
* **Brief Description:** A blue and yellow capsule (pill) 34, 36\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Bronze Benefactor Box Prize (Common rarity, but highly advanced tech) 35, 37-39.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Permanently alters the user's brain and wetware interface. The first pill granted Carl the ability to identify and visually analyze Subspace Portals 35, 37\. A second pill installed a permanent altimeter and airspeed indicator into his vision 40\.

**Gorgon Marital Aid**

* **Brief History:** Awarded in a Silver Mechanic's Box. Carl used it alongside the Goo-Inator to build precise plungers for his train-derailing landmines 41, 42\.  
* **Brief Description:** A tool shaped like a spatula 41, 43\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Silver Mechanic's Box Prize 41, 43\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** A hardening and de-hardening tool used at workbenches. It assists in fusing joints or creating varying degrees of plasticity in rigid materials without affecting their overall material strength 44, 45\.

**Stick and Poke Tattoo Kit**

* **Brief History:** Awarded in a Gold Quest box for the *Vengeance of the Daughter* quest. It was used by Edgar the tortoise to put stabilizing memory tattoos onto Tsarina Signet 46, 47\.  
* **Brief Description:** A basic tattoo tool set 48\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Gold Quest Box Prize 46\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Allows an artist to create a tattoo on a willing or unwilling target. Depending on the artist's skill and the ink used, the tattoos can imbue magical buffs, upgrades, or hold trapped spirits and memories 5, 48\.

**Enchanted Gnome FarSeerer**

* **Brief History:** Looted as an accessory for airships and bomber vehicles 49\.  
* **Brief Description:** A spyglass or telescope-like targeting device 49, 50\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Airship Accessory / Vehicle Tech 49\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** When installed on a dreadnaught or airship, it increases the accuracy of gravity-based bombs by 75%. It allows the user to inspect and gather system information on objects from a great distance 49\.

**Borough Field Guide / Neighborhood Map**

* **Brief History:** Looted from various Borough or Neighborhood bosses (like the tusklings and Gore-Gore) 51, 52\.  
* **Brief Description:** Informational map items 51, 52\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Boss Loot / Persistent Item 51, 52\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** The Neighborhood map populates the user's interface with the immediate area layout or train lines. The Field Guide actively adds monster types to the map, revealing what enemies are lurking in the dark before the crawler encounters them 51-53.

**Enchanted Xistera Extension Slot**

* **Brief History:** Custom-made by the Pacifist Network so Carl could throw the possessed doll head (Samantha) at the god Diwata 54, 55\.  
* **Brief Description:** A curved piece of glowing wicker basket that attaches directly to the end of Carl's xistera scoop 54, 56\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Unique / Platinum Sponsor Box Prize 54\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Custom-fitted to hold decapitated love doll heads. Dramatically increases the distance and force with which a withering spirit's head can be thrown 54\.

**Gordon’s Compass of Vertical Labyrinth Management**

* **Brief History:** Received by Everly in a benefactor box from Dictum Waystation Controls. It purposefully led her straight into a corporate advertisement trap tower 57, 58\.  
* **Brief Description:** A navigational compass with 49 uses 57\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Benefactor Box Prize 57\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Points the user toward specific vertical targets or stairwells, though it can be manipulated by sponsors to lead crawlers into dangerous corporate traps 57, 58\.

**Steam Engineer's Key / Ochre Line Engineer's Key**

* **Brief History:** Looted by Carl from train engineers during the Iron Tangle 52, 59\.  
* **Brief Description:** Large, bronze keys 59, 60\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** NPC Loot 52, 59\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Allows access to employee-only areas, maintenance tunnels, and actions on the railway lines of the Iron Tangle 59, 60\.

**Enchanted Fur Brush of the Ecclesiastic**

* **Brief History:** Part of Donut's extensive grooming collection 61\.  
* **Brief Description:** A pet grooming brush 61\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Magic Accessory 61\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Grants the user a large temporary Constitution buff if someone physically brushes them with it for 10 minutes a day 61\.

**Trans Tunnel 7C Orator Relay**

* **Brief History:** Placed in Carl's inventory by Valtay Corp, but the AI immediately flagged it as an illegal, non-sanctioned item 62, 63\.  
* **Brief Description:** An inter-tunnel communications device manufactured by Valtay Corp 62, 64\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Tech-based device / Contraband 63, 65\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Intended to bypass dungeon communication blackouts, but attempting to use or circumvent dungeon rules with it results in immediate disqualification by the System AI 63, 65\.
