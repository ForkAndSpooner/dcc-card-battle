Here are the item cards for the Toys, Collectibles, and Summoning Plushies featured in the sources, ordered from the most prominently featured to the least.  
**Veriluxx RealPet Companion "Princess Donut" Special Edition (Robot Donut)**

* **Brief History:** Sent to Donut as a "benefactor box" (actually a product sample) from the toy company Veriluxx. Mongo repeatedly attacked and destroyed the early models, causing them to violently explode 1-3.  
* **Brief Description:** A slightly undersized, robotic toy version of Princess Donut with off-color, rat-like fur 1\. It constantly spits out bootleg Garfield quotes like "I sure do like lasagna" and "I hate Mondays, Carl" 4\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Collectible Toy (Dungeon Crawl Special Edition) 4\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** An interactive toy featuring an onboard AI preprogrammed with a supposedly "accurate" persona 5\. The dungeon-enhanced version contains a tamper-proof Zentix polymer back panel that causes the toy to act as a self-destructing bomb (exploding in a shower of sparks and smoke) if broken or illegally modified 2, 6\.

**Stuffed Kimaris Figure (Jaxbrin Amusements)**

* **Brief History:** Looted by Carl from Miss Quill's office. It survived a massive explosion because it was housed inside a protective Sheol Glass Reaper Case 7, 8\.  
* **Brief Description:** A collectible stuffed bean bag depicting a scowling, armored man atop a black horse 7, 9\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Unique (Incredibly High Value) 10\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** When the tag is removed, it performs a "true summoning" of the demon Kimaris (one of the brothers of Sheol) to fight. Because of its Unique rarity, the demon will remain summoned for the entire remainder of the floor 11, 12\.

**Stuffed Grulke Infantry Figure (Jaxbrin Amusements)**

* **Brief History:** Daniel Bautista's primary, go-to plushie weapon. He gave one to Carl to explain how his class fighting style worked 13\.  
* **Brief Description:** A brown frog wearing a spiked helmet (with several armored variations) 13, 14\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Common (Bottom tier rarity) 13\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** When the tag is ripped off like a grenade pin, it takes ten seconds for the toy to transform into a real, fighting Grulke Infantry frog 15\. Because it is a common rarity, the summon only lasts between fifteen to thirty seconds before reverting back into a tagless toy 15, 16\.

**Stuffed Sage Sprite Lecturer Figure "Atwin" (Jaxbrin Amusements)**

* **Brief History:** One of the first rare plushies Bautista summoned. Taking the form of an elderly teacher, Atwin taught Bautista how to properly read the stats, rarities, and abilities of his other plush toys 17\.  
* **Brief Description:** A stuffed sprite kept in a glass display case 17\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Legendary Rare (1 of 5\) 17\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** When summoned, he lasted for two whole hours and provided invaluable tactical knowledge and system mechanics tutoring 17\.

**Stuffed Back Alley Mouser (Jaxbrin Amusements)**

* **Brief History:** Bautista used this plushie to create a decoy of Princess Donut to trick the elite crawler Lucia Mar 14, 18\.  
* **Brief Description:** A fuzzy cat plush that Bautista customized by dressing it in a fake tiara and sunglasses 14\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Collectible Summon 14\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Summons a feline creature. Bautista had to cast a *Tame* spell on it before pulling the tag to prevent it from instantly attacking his own party, allowing him to use it as a highly effective distraction 19, 20\.

**Stuffed Slate Butterfly Figure (Jaxbrin Amusements)**

* **Brief History:** Bautista received over 100 of these directly from his sponsor after doing a commercial for them 21\.  
* **Brief Description:** A gray and brown, fuzzy moth or butterfly toy 14, 21\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Common 21\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Transforms into a flying Slate Butterfly monster when the tag is pulled 14\.

**Stuffed Eclipse Pyxies (Jaxbrin Amusements)**

* **Brief Description:** Stuffed animals that look like fat, fur-covered guinea pigs with bat wings and jagged teeth 14\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Collectible Summon 14\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Transforms into a swarm of aggressive, flying demonic pyxies 14, 22\.

**Veriluxx RealPet Companion "Mongo"**

* **Brief Description:** A toy designed to mimic the juvenile stage of the Mongoliensis dinosaur, standing about half the height of the Robot Donut 23\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Collectible Toy 5\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** An interactive toy that mimics the real Mongo. During a commercial shoot, it accurately replicated Mongo's early behavior by immediately jumping up and biting the Robot Donut directly on the nose 23\.

**Stuffed Flame-ingo Figure (Jaxbrin Amusements)**

* **Brief Description:** A pink flamingo stuffed animal 24\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Common 24\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Summons a flamingo monster when the tag is pulled. It is noted as a favorite among the more feminine fans of Jaxbrin toys 25\.

**Stuffed Crane Crasher (Jaxbrin Amusements)**

* **Brief History:** The very first plush Bautista ever summoned. He ripped the tag off in a saferoom, and the bird flew to the ceiling, screamed, attacked a party member, and teleported away 17\.  
* **Brief Description:** A bird-like stuffed animal 17\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Collectible Summon 17\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Summons a hostile, screaming bird creature 17\.

**Stuffed Tummy Achers (Jaxbrin Amusements)**

* **Brief Description:** Round, knee-high meatball-like pets 14\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Collectible Summon 14\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Summons Belly Achers into combat. Bautista carried a pair of them but hesitated to use them because he suspected they would spawn as massive versions of the creature 26\.

**Veriluxx RealPet Companion "Uzi Jesus"**

* **Brief History:** Kept on the shelf of the failing Saccathian shopkeeper, Zander, right before the 12th floor collapsed 27\.  
* **Brief Description:** An action figure of the famous totem, Uzi Jesus 28\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Collectible Toy 27\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** When tapped, it comes to life and speaks pre-recorded catchphrases like "Hello. I am Uzi Jesus. You look quite upset. What’s the problem, son?" and "Don't look at my dick, lady\!" 27, 28\.

**Veriluxx RealPet Companion "Raul the Crab"**

* **Brief Description:** An action figure of Donut's crustacean totem, Raul 28\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Collectible Toy 27\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** When knocked over, it comes to life and shouts phrases like "Hello, fine warrior\! I am Raul" and "Do not kowtow. Never kowtow\!" 28\.

**Other Veriluxx RealPet Companions**

* **Brief Description:** Action figures based on the totems Gimli, Geraldo, and Skyler Spinach 27\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Collectible Toy 27\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Unpopular toy shelf-warmers that act as physical merchandise for the Faction Wars totems 27\.
