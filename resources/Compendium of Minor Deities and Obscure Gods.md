Here are the additional missing gods and minor deities mentioned throughout the sources, filling in the gaps from the previous list. Because many of these are obscure or only briefly mentioned by the characters, some details regarding their visual appearance, voices, or specific sponsors remain unknown within the provided context.  
**Algos**

* **Brief Backstory:** The God of Pain. He is considered a highly destructive deity to summon, as he would kill everyone in a town in seconds to ensure maximum suffering 1\.  
* **Key Relationships:** Crown Prince Stalwart and Prince Maestro of the Skull Empire provided a scroll allowing the player-killer Maggie My to worship him 2\.  
* **Sponsorship Status:** While Algos himself isn't explicitly stated to be sponsored, Maggie My is sponsored by the Skull Empire, who orchestrated her connection to this god 2\.  
* **Visual Description:** Not fully described, though his picture is featured on a choice box 2\.  
* **Unique Powers:** Specializes in widespread pain and suffering, capable of wiping out whole towns almost instantly 1\.  
* **Voice:** Unknown.

**Quetzalcoatlus**

* **Brief Backstory:** A vulture goddess who reigns as a ghost queen haunting the necropolis of Anser on the fifth floor 3, 4\. The glass wizard Ghazi accidentally allowed the minor deity Psamathe (Samantha) to usurp and hide within Quetzalcoatlus's ghost form when attempting to summon a different god from the Nothing 4, 5\.  
* **Key Relationships:** Usurped by the withering spirit of Psamathe 4\.  
* **Sponsorship Status:** Not sponsored.  
* **Visual Description:** A dinosaur-like ghost of a massive pterodactyl with a South-American aesthetic 3\.  
* **Unique Powers:** A powerful ghost boss capable of manifesting within the necropolis 4, 6\.  
* **Voice:** Unknown.

**Makara (or Maki / Maraka)**

* **Brief Backstory:** A goddess trapped in the Nothing. She is quite the entrepreneur, having set up a little sushi stand in the terrifying alternate dimension 7\.  
* **Key Relationships:** Interacted with Samantha in the Nothing 7\.  
* **Sponsorship Status:** Not sponsored (Banished to the Nothing).  
* **Visual Description:** She has the head of a crocodile and the body of a fish 7\.  
* **Unique Powers:** She makes sushi rolls out of her own innards, which magically keep growing back so she can continually harvest them to sell. The sushi tastes terrible 7\.  
* **Voice:** Unknown.

**Capa**

* **Brief Backstory:** A beaver god who is also trapped in the Nothing. He is perpetually miserable and always crying 8\.  
* **Key Relationships:** Bullied by another god, Hehaka 8\.  
* **Sponsorship Status:** Not sponsored (Banished to the Nothing).  
* **Visual Description:** A beaver 8\.  
* **Unique Powers:** Unknown.  
* **Voice:** He is always crying 8\.

**Hehaka**

* **Brief Backstory:** Another deity trapped within the Nothing who bullies the beaver god Capa 8\.  
* **Key Relationships:** Uses Capa as a clothing accessory 8\.  
* **Sponsorship Status:** Not sponsored (Banished to the Nothing).  
* **Visual Description:** Unknown, other than wearing a beaver as a hat 8\.  
* **Unique Powers:** Unknown.  
* **Voice:** Unknown.

**Enyo**

* **Brief Backstory:** A goddess from the "first enlightenment," back when deities aggressively competed for worshippers. She has an order of militant nuns who travel to villages to proselytize. If the villagers refuse to convert, the nuns drop a "Celestial Grenade" to summon Enyo to aggressively change their minds 9\.  
* **Key Relationships:** Served by an aggressive order of nuns 9\.  
* **Sponsorship Status:** Unknown.  
* **Visual Description:** Unknown.  
* **Unique Powers:** Can be summoned via Celestial Grenades for 60 seconds of divine intervention 9, 10\.  
* **Voice:** Unknown.
