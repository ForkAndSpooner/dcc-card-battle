Here is a list of the various buffs, debuffs, and diseases found across the sources, detailing their effects and how the characters acquired or interacted with them.

### Buffs

* **Freeballing**: Grants an additional 100% damage bonus to all attacks that originate below the waist, and those skills train 20% faster, but it restricts the user to a single layer of crotch armor 1\. Carl received this permanently by using a short-shelf-life Scroll of Upgrade on his Enchanted BigBoi Boxers 1, 2\.  
* **Cockroach**: A survival skill that saves the user from a fatal blow by rapidly healing them or providing a shield 3, 4\. Donut received this automatically upon choosing the "Former Child Actor" class, and the Bedlam Bride (Shi Maria) also possessed it 4, 5\.  
* **Magical Fervor**: Triples the user's mana points, increases strength, makes them glow blue, and renders them immune to Fear spells 6\. It was activated by City Elf Temple Recruitment Wand Bearers when they ate magical acorns 6\.  
* **Unbreakable / Celestially Nimble and Tidy**: The Unbreakable buff keeps the user's foot bones (metatarsals) from snapping under heavy weight, while Nimble and Tidy makes it so traps triggered by footfalls sound an alarm with a 5-second delay before detonating 7, 8\. Carl acquired these by using the Enchanted Pedicure Kit of the Sylph for 15 minutes in his safe room 7, 9\.  
* **Good Rest / Great Rest**: Adds a 10% (Good Rest) or 15% (Great Rest) bonus to all base stats, experience, and skill training for a 30-hour period 10, 11\. Carl and Donut gained this by sleeping in upgraded personal space beds like the Instacot 60 10, 11\.  
* **You are full\! (Warm Tummy)**: Increases healing speed by 5%, decreases debuff times by 5%, and lowers indirect damage by 5% 12\. Carl obtained this buff after eating a microwave biscuit sandwich dispensed by his kitchen upgrade 12\.  
* **Titivated**: Reduces damage taken from incoming projectiles 13\. Carl received this when defensive mages stacked protection spells on him in the Faction Wars trenches 13\.  
* **Soothed\!**: Decreases pain and makes all active debuffs 10% less effective 14\. The vampire Miriam Dom cast this on Carl to help him recover after a brutal fight 14\.  
* **Juiced\! / Shrouded\! / Trolled\!**: A stacked buff that gives \+10% strength, 10% damage reduction, and a 25% acceleration to healing 15\. Carl and his allies received these buffs whenever Imani wrapped them in an ethereal hug with her magical butterfly wings 15, 16\.  
* **Limbered\!**: Increases the party's dexterity by 50% 17\. Donut bestowed this buff upon the party by singing an autotuned bard spell using her Golden Throat microphone 17, 18\.  
* **Immortal**: Prevents a health bar from forming and keeps the user from dying or losing limbs to otherwise fatal blows, though they still feel the pain of being crushed or burned 19, 20\. Carl and Vinata were temporarily granted this buff simply by standing in the presence of the god Khepri 19\.  
* **Shining Charisma / Speedster**: Shining Charisma grants party members 20% of your charisma, and Speedster allows for extremely fast short-distance movement (which Carl combined with his Gloom Wraith Phase) 21, 22\. Carl received these buffs after brushing his long hair for five minutes with the screaming Enchanted Hairbrush of the Beefmaster 21\.  
* **Closed Off\!**: Makes the user immune to all aura effects, including both Blessings and Dreads 23\. Carl gained this by drinking a specific potion to protect himself from Imani's and enemies' auras 23\.  
* **Enraged**: Causes the target to go berserk and viciously attack their own allies 24\. A mantis hunter was afflicted with this after Carl threw a berserking potion directly at her back 24\.

### Debuffs & Diseases

* **Shit-Faced**: Gives a 50% chance to render the drinker blind for 30 hours, a 5% chance of instant death, and a 45% chance to permanently raise a random stat by two points 25\. This is inflicted by taking a 1.5-ounce swig of Rev-Up Toilet-Grade Moonshine 25\.  
* **Buzzed (Shaky Cam / Truth Serum)**: Grants \+3 Charisma and \-1 Dexterity, while the Shaky Cam and Truth Serum components make the user dizzy, woozy, and uncoordinated 26-28. Carl contracted this by drinking the foul-tasting Rev-Up Immunity Smoothie 26, 27\.  
* **Sepsis / Septic**: Operates similarly to poison, inflicting rapid damage over time, but it doesn't stack and can be healed normally 29, 30\. Donut inflicted this on enemies using the Enchanted Crown of the Sepsis Whore and later her tiara 29, 30\.  
* **Poisoned / Potion Sickness**: Plummets health rapidly and causes severe stomach pain; Potion Sickness prevents the crawler from taking any more potions for a set duration 31-33. Carl accidentally poisoned himself and got Potion Sickness by drinking two potions back-to-back before his cooldown timer had finished 31-33.  
* **The Taint**: Balances the victim on the precipice of death by completely preventing them from healing their health using any method 34, 35\. Carl contracted this disease after being clawed by Unvaccinated Clurichaun Rev-Up Consultants 34, 35\.  
* **Queasy**: Causes violent, uncontrollable vomiting 36, 37\. Carl and Donut were afflicted with this physical/auditory attack when fighting a group of walking rabbit monsters 36, 37\.  
* **Where the Fuck Am I? Who the Fuck Are You?**: Causes complete memory and control loss, making the user forget where they are or why they are fighting 38, 39\. It is a dangerous side effect of drinking the Potion of Bloodlust 38\.  
* **Despondent**: Lowers all stats by 15% and causes the sufferer to take 10% more damage from worshippers of other deities 40\. The dwarf Arief and other NPCs suffered this debuff permanently after their sponsored goddess, Ysalte, was killed 40, 41\.  
* **Soul Poisoning / Post Nut Clarity**: Causes worsening jolts of burning pain, and the "Post Nut Clarity" side effect forcibly knocked Carl unconscious immediately after he activated a powerful skill 42-44. Carl suffered this after the Bedlam Bride (Shi Maria) was tattooed onto his chest and began trying to force her way out 42, 44, 45\.  
* **Deshrouded**: Instantly strips the target of all active War Protections, illusions, and smoke cloaks 46\. The enemy Dream faction cast this over Carl's entire army during the Faction Wars to expose them 46\.  
* **Skanked**: Lowers Charisma by 70%, causes the victim to stink horrifically, and induces violent vomiting for three hours 47, 48\. Carl's squad (including Donut and the elves) contracted this after being sprayed by a Fancy Boy Skank Skunk 47, 48\.  
* **Endless Thread of Distraction / Self Harm / Intrusive Thoughts**: Overwhelming psionic mind-magic debuffs that flood the user with intense feelings of doubt, failure, and suicidal thoughts 49\. Carl was bombarded with these by enemy war mages 49\.  
* **Fragile**: Drops the sufferer to exactly 1 HP, meaning the slightest amount of physical damage will kill them instantly 50, 51\. Prepotente was afflicted with this after a stun spell violently ping-ponged back and forth between two reflection rings 50\.  
* **Cursed Light Walker**: Causes health to slowly seep away whenever the victim is in the dark/away from light 52\. Prepotente accidentally inflicted this on Viscount Fog as a mutated version of the vampire curse 52\.  
* **Left to Fester\!**: Prevents all healing, leaving the user to suffer the pain of their injuries until their specific target is killed 53, 54\. Carl and Xindy received this automatically upon marking targets with the Ring of Divine Suffering 54, 55\.  
* **Immortalized**: Halves strength and mana and prevents healing while the target is in direct sunlight, though it turns into a massive buff in moonlight 56, 57\. Dinosaurs (like the velociraptors and Big Tina) received this debuff after being cured of vampirism 56, 57\.  
* **Sore as Shit**: Causes generalized pain across the entire body for hours without directly affecting stats 58\. Applied automatically to Carl after surviving massive, severe physical trauma 58\.  
* **Blood Trail**: Health seeps downward, open wounds continue to weep blood allowing the victim to be tracked, and the victim gives off a pungent smell 58\. Carl received this from a hunter's ranged weapon attack 58\.  
* **Temporary Amnesia / Suffering Bleed**: Silently drains health down to 10% (or 1% on a second strike) while hiding the cause of death from the system 59\. Caused by the assassin weapon "Dirty Little Phased Bolt" wielded by Hekla against Katia 59\.  
* **Bonked**: A crowd control stagger debuff 60\. It is imparted randomly by striking enemies with the sapient nickel sock weapon, Spunky Jefferson 60\.  
* **Under Orders**: Compels the victim to obey the caster's commands and prevents them from harming the caster 61\. Carl inflicted this on Lucia Mar by smashing a Hell-kissed Conscription Potion on her head 61, 62\.  
* **The Gurgles (Insanity / Rabies)**: A nasty, non-curable, highly contagious biological disease that drives the victim completely insane 63, 64\. Reavers injected this into their Juiced Rot Sticker Behemoths to turn them into living, biological missiles 63, 65\.  
* **Shunned / Penitence**: Doubles tithing requirements and threatens an imminent divine smite 66\. Carl received this from the god Emberus after accidentally killing an NPC cleric who also worshipped Emberus 66, 67\.  
* **Mute**: Completely prevents the target from casting spells 68\. Donut uses this via a spell tome (and later containment barrels) to silence enemy casters 68, 69\.  
* **Shelved**: Freezes the target completely in place for several minutes 70\. The god Ogun used this on Katia with a wave of his hand to temporarily stop her from attacking him 70\.  
* **Ouch**: A stagger/stun attack similar to getting kicked in the groin 71\. Carl gained a 15% chance to inflict this with every foot-based attack via his "Stepson" character class upgrade 71\.  
* **Unsettled**: Causes discomfort and disorientation 72\. Inflicted on bug enemies when Carl threw a music-playing trap ball at them 72\.  
* **Exhaust-ed**: Knocks targets unconscious until they take physical damage 73\. Inflicted by Louis's overpowered Cloud of Exhaust spell 73, 74\.  
* **Your dick will fall off if you get an erection**: A highly specific, terrifying temporary anatomical threat 75\. Inflicted on Carl just by standing in the presence of the feral god Meatus 75\.  
* **Vinegar**: Turns any potions handled outside of the inventory directly into vinegar (which restores to a more powerful version upon the god's banishment) 76\. Temporarily inflicted on crawlers simply by being in the presence of the goddess Ysalte 76\.  
* **Disoriented**: Causes tumbling and dizziness 77\. Inflicted on the hunter Akland after being struck by three different magic bolts simultaneously 77\.
