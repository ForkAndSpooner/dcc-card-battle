Here are the item cards for the armor, clothing, and defensive accessories featured in the sources, ordered from the most prominently featured to the least.  
**Enchanted BigBoi Boxers**

* **Brief History:** Awarded to Carl from a boss box, he later used a Scroll of Upgrade on them. The upgrade unexpectedly granted the "Freeballing" benefit, which stripped him of his pants for the remainder of the dungeon 1-4.  
* **Brief Description:** A self-sizing white pair of boxers covered in little red hearts 4-6.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Boss Box Prize (Upgraded once) 1, 2\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Adds \+5 Dexterity, \+5 Intelligence, and \+5 Constitution (total) 3\. Allows the wearer to cast a level 15 *Protective Shell* once every 30 hours 3, 6\. The *Freeballing* benefit grants an additional 100% damage bonus to all attacks originating below the waist, and those skills train 20% faster, but it restricts the user to a single layer of crotch armor 3, 7\.

**The Cloak of the Benevolent Champion**

* **Brief History:** Originally awarded in a Celestial Quest Box on the third floor, it was looted by Carl after he ripped the arm off the player-killer Quan Ch 8, 9\. Quan had notoriously used it to hunt and execute weak crawlers, twisting the item's benevolent purpose 10\. Carl later gave it to Princess Donut 9\.  
* **Brief Description:** A silky, glowing cloak that is black on one side and blue on the other, featuring ethereal, wisp-like wings 8, 11\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Celestial / Unique 12\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** The cloak's powers are custom-tailored to the wearer but generally include flight, hovering, and heavy magical shields 9, 13\. It slowly raises the user's lowest base stat to equal their highest base stat over 66 hours 14\. It immediately upgrades one random spell to level 15 (and three others over a week) and upgrades one random skill to level 15 15\. It also grants the *Find Weakened* skill 14\.

**Enchanted Anarchist’s Battle Rattle**

* **Brief History:** Awarded to Carl from a platinum Spicy box, it finally allowed him to replace his burned and ruined pre-dungeon leather jacket 16, 17\.  
* **Brief Description:** A sleeveless, black canvas jacket that glows with a subtle purple light. It is specifically designed to host upgrade patches 5, 16, 18\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Platinum Box Prize 16\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Adds \+1 to all base stats, and this base stat upgrade increases by \+1 for every compatible patch added (+2 for an eligible Back Patch) 19\. It grants access to the Desperado Club and the Naughty Boys Employment Agency 19\. It also grants \+50% range and accuracy for all thrown explosives 19\.

**The Scavenger’s Daughter Patch (Back Patch)**

* **Brief History:** Awarded from a Celestial Predator Box, this divine patch caused Carl immense stress due to its connections to the gods and its potential to draw their ire 20-22.  
* **Brief Description:** A thick, black patch embroidered with a white pyramid of skulls, sewn with clear fiberoptic-like stitching 21\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Celestial / Divine / Unique / Fleeting 23\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Doubles all patch stat point benefits on his jacket 24\. Adds \+25 Dexterity and \+25 Strength 24\. It notifies the user whenever a deity enters the area 24\. Grants *Climb*, *Soul Reaper* (stores essence from melee kills to unleash a massive, varied attack), and *Mysterious Bone Key* (uses a bone from the user's body to permanently open any lock, destroying the jacket and patches in the process) 24-26. It imbues varying benefits in the presence of divine entities 22, 23\.

**Enchanted Trollskin Shirt of Pummeling**

* **Brief History:** Found in a Gold Apparel Box early in the dungeon, it quickly became Carl's primary under-armor 27, 28\.  
* **Brief Description:** A stylish, short-sleeved, form-fitting under-armor shirt 28-30.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Gold Apparel Box Prize 31\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Grants \+7 to the Regeneration skill (rapid healing) 27\. Negates melee-based damage debuffs like Stun, Knockback, Disarm, and Out-of-Breath 28\. Unlike most monster-skin items, it does not trigger negative reactions from trolls 28\.

**Enchanted Nightgaunt Cloak of Stoutness**

* **Brief Description:** A black, leathery cloak made from demon wings, stretched between rows of long fingerbones, featuring pointed ears on the hood 5, 32\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Gold Apparel Box Prize 31\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Grants \+4 Constitution 27\. Provides resistance to poison and ice-based attacks, and adds Anti-Piercing resistance to all worn armor 27\. Pisses off any Nightgaunts who see it 27\.

**Enchanted Spiked Kneepads of the Shade Gnoll Riot Forces**

* **Brief Description:** Slip-on knee bracers that feature retractable, needle-like eight-inch spikes 5, 33\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Gold Crowd Control Box Prize 34\.

**Enchanted Spiked Knee pads the Munificent Goddess Kina**

As a divine item, it imbues varying benefits in the presence of deities 36\.

**Enchanted Crown of the Sepsis Whore**
