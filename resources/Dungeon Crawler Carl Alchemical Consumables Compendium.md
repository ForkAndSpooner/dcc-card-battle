Here are the item cards for the potions, drinks, and related alchemical consumables featured in the sources, ordered from the most prominently featured to the least.  
**Mordecai’s Special Brew**

* **Brief History:** Formulated by Mordecai to save crawlers in dire emergencies, Carl relied on this brew to survive instant-death blows from bosses like Grull and Shi Maria 1, 2\.  
* **Brief Description:** A powerful alchemical potion created by the shifty Changeling manager 3, 4\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** High Value / Custom Crafted 3, 4\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Combines a Gold Standard Healing Potion and the Troll's Second Wind to create a constant stream of healing and rapid regeneration, granting de facto immortality for 30 seconds 3-6. As a severe side effect, it adds ten hours to the user's potion sickness countdown, and a crawler can only ever drink two of these in their lifetime before they stop working completely 5, 6\.

**Standard / Fine Healing Potions & Mana Potions**

* **Brief History:** The basic currency of survival in the dungeon, Carl and Donut constantly hoard these to survive brutal encounters 7-10.  
* **Brief Description:** Red liquids for healing and blue liquids for mana, often drunk manually or automatically triggered via hotlists 7-10.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Common to Rare (Quality ranges from Standard to Fine and Gold-Standard) 3, 7, 9\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Healing potions restore massive percentages of health rapidly, while Mana potions restore spell points (some instantly, others like "Good Mana Refills" restore points slowly over two minutes) 7, 9, 11\. Using two potions back-to-back before the cooldown expires inflicts "Potion Sickness" 12\.

**Rev-Up Toilet-Grade Moonshine**

* **Brief History:** Produced by the Rev-Up Laminak elites on the second floor, Carl hoarded dozens of these jugs to use as highly explosive accelerants for his custom "Jug-O-Booms" 13-18.  
* **Brief Description:** A 180-proof, highly flammable liquid in a clay jug 14, 17\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** High Value / Dungeon Consumable 14, 17, 19, 20\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Taking a 1.5-ounce swig inflicts the *Shit-Faced* debuff, with a 50% chance to render the drinker blind for 30 hours, a 5% chance of instant death, and a 45% chance to permanently raise a random stat by two points 14, 17\. Its fumes are highly explosive 14, 17\.

**Rev-Up Immunity Smoothie**

* **Brief History:** Carl created this foul mixture on a bicycle-powered blender while fighting a tentacle boss, frantically shoving a dead fairy into the glass to complete the recipe 21-24.  
* **Brief Description:** A pink, sparkling liquid consisting of one part moonshine and one part blended Laminak fairy corpse 22, 24-26.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Quest-Specific Crafted Drink 21, 23\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Provides 10 doses that grant temporary immunity to health-seeping conditions and communicable diseases (like the Taint and Vigorous Measles) 25-28. It inflicts the *Buzzed* debuff, granting \+3 Charisma, \-1 Dexterity, *Shaky Cam*, and *Truth Serum* 25, 26\.

**Hell-Kissed Potion of Conscription**

* **Brief History:** Li Na duplicated this incredibly rare potion and used the original to successfully conscript the god Harpocrates to her side 29, 30\. Carl later attempted to use his duplicated copy on Prepotente 31\.  
* **Brief Description:** A bubbling purple liquid housed in a screaming glass bottle shaped like a demonic, eyeless head with waving tentacles 32\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Level 18 / Extremely Rare (Brewed by Krakaren Prime) 33\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Forces unaffiliated entities, including most deities, to be conscripted into the user's faction or the War Mage Rebellion 33\. It is highly dangerous to cast if the level discrepancy between the user and the target is too high, causing the user to detonate 29, 34\.

**Invisibility Potion**

* **Brief History:** A staple for survival, utilized constantly by Carl to sneak past elite hunters, escape bosses, and bypass deadly traps 35-39.  
* **Brief Description:** A clear consumable liquid 35\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Expensive / Rare 40\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Renders the user invisible for a short duration, usually calculating to the user's Intelligence stat multiplied by two seconds 41\.

**Dolores Doesn’t Splat Potion / Half Splat Potion**

* **Brief History:** Carl and Donut used this gruesome but effective concoction to survive plummeting down a massive vertical drop at high speeds 42, 43\.  
* **Brief Description:** A liquid cocktail combining rock buffalo breeding magic and Katia's *Crowd Blast* invulnerability mechanics 43\.  
* **Rarity/Value Level:** Uncommon / Alchemist Crafted 43, 44\.  
* **Powers and Abilities:** Guarantees survival from terminal falls by vastly accelerating fall speed and softening the ground upon impact, effectively vaporizing the landing surface 43, 45\. The cheaper "Half Splat" variant leaves the user alive but at 5% health 46\.

**Hell-Kissed Celestial Skill Potion of the Prince of Woe**

* **Rarity/Value Level:** Celestial 48\.

**Pawna's Tears / Pawna's Cries**
